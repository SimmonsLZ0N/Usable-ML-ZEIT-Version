Understanding CNNs

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) are at the heart of modern AI, powering advancements in computer vision. From facial recognition to medical image analysis, CNNs help machines see and understand our world.

Our website focuses on making CNNs accessible and understandable. Through interactive visualizations and intuitive explanations, we demystify how these networks learn to recognize patterns and make sense of images.
